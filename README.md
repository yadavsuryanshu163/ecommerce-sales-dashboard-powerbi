# 📊 Suryanshu Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

## Overview

The **Suryanshu Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ecommerce sales performance. It provides insights into revenue, profit, order quantity, customer purchases, payment methods, product categories, and monthly profit trends, enabling data-driven business decisions.

## Key Metrics (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the most important business metrics at the top:

| KPI                           | Description                              |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Sum of Amount**             | Total sales revenue generated (**438K**) |
| **Sum of Quantity**           | Total products sold (**6K units**)       |
| **Sum of Profit**             | Overall profit earned (**37K**)          |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | Average revenue per order (**121K**)     |

---

## Dashboard Features

### 📅 Quarter & State Filters

* Filter dashboard data by **Quarter (Q1–Q4)**.
* Analyze sales for a specific **State**.
* All visuals update dynamically based on selected filters.

---

### 📈 Monthly Profit Analysis

Displays monthly profit performance throughout the year.

**Insights**

* Highest profit observed in **November**.
* Negative profit recorded during **May**, **July**, **September**, and **December**.
* Useful for identifying seasonal trends and loss-making periods.

---

### 💰 Payment Method Distribution

Shows the percentage of quantity sold through different payment methods.

* **Cash on Delivery (COD): 44%**
* **UPI: 21%**
* **Debit Card: 13%**
* **Credit Card: 12%**
* **EMI: 10%**

This helps understand customer payment preferences.

---

### 🛍️ Sales by Category

Displays quantity sold across major product categories.

* **Clothing – 63%**
* **Electronics – 21%**
* **Furniture – 17%**

Useful for identifying the best-performing product categories.

---

### 📦 Profit by Sub-Category

Ranks product sub-categories based on generated profit.

Top performing sub-categories include:

* Printers
* Bookcases
* Sarees
* Accessories
* Tables

Helps identify the most profitable products.

---

### 📊 Profit by State

Compares **Sales Amount** and **Profit** across different states.

This visual helps identify:

* High-revenue states
* High-profit states
* Regions requiring business improvement

---

### 👥 Sales by Customer

Shows the top customers based on total purchase amount.

This helps:

* Identify high-value customers
* Support loyalty programs
* Focus customer retention strategies

---

## Business Insights

* Strong sales performance with **438K** total revenue.
* Clothing contributes the largest share of total sales.
* **Cash on Delivery** remains the preferred payment method.
* Profit varies significantly across months, indicating seasonal demand.
* Certain product sub-categories generate substantially higher profits than others.
* Customer and regional analysis helps identify top-performing markets and valuable customers.
